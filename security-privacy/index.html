Security and Privacy at Blueprint

We built this platform to support trusted relationships between communities and capital stewards. That includes clear commitments about privacy, data use, access control, and security.

Our commitment

Blueprint is infrastructure for movement finance, not a data brokerage business. Our approach is simple: your information belongs to you and your organization, and we protect it accordingly.

We do not sell your data

We do not sell personal data, organizational data, giving history, or relationship data. Your information is not sold to advertisers, brokers, or other third parties.

We do not use your data to train AI

Data from your account, organization, and platform activity is not used to train generative AI or machine learning models. We use data only to operate and improve your product experience.

You control who has access

Organizations manage access through roles and permissions. You control who is invited, who can view information, and who can take administrative actions.

  • Role-based access by team function
  • Organization-level member management
  • Ability to update or remove access as teams change

High security standards

We implement layered safeguards across infrastructure, application design, and operations, including encryption, access controls, monitoring, and secure development practices.

No internet platform can guarantee absolute security, but we apply the highest practical level of protection we can and continuously improve our controls.

Questions

If your team needs additional details about security or privacy expectations, contact us through the contact form and we can walk through your requirements.
