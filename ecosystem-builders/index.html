What you get

Four tools.
One connected network.

01 — Ecosystem Mapping

A live map of your network

Every organization in your network gets a Blueprint profile with its story, capital needs, impact data, and fundraising readiness in one place. You see the full picture. Funders see it too.

02 — Visibility

Network visibility to funders

Blueprint connects your member organizations to aligned foundations, DAFs, and advisors already looking to deploy. Your network's capital needs become visible to the right people without relying on warm intros alone.

03 — Coordination

Coordinated fundraising

Member organizations can define needs across grants, loans, recoverable grants, and equity. Blueprint helps them build a capital plan if they do not already have one, while you see patterns across the whole network and coordinate instead of compete.

04 — Reporting

Shared impact reporting

Aggregate impact data across your member organizations in one view. Generate reports that show the collective outcomes of your network for funders, governance, and public narrative without assembling everything by hand.

Why it matters

The coordination gap is
costing your network.

Your members are doing important work, but their capital needs are often scattered across decks, applications, spreadsheets, and conversations. That makes the full picture hard to see and even harder to fund.

Ecosystem builders end up manually brokering relationships that could be systematic. You are already the connective tissue, but without shared infrastructure the work stays trapped in inboxes and memory.

Blueprint closes both gaps by giving every member organization a voice in the same system and giving you a live view of the whole ecosystem.
