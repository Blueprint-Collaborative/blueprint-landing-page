Your public profile

Always free

Capital plan support

Not sure how to define your funding needs? Blueprint walks you through it. We help you think through what capital you need, in what form, and when. You don't need a plan to start. We'll build one with you.

Guided tool

Impact reporting

Keep your community and funders connected to your progress. Update your metrics in Blueprint and they flow directly into the reports that foundations, advisors, and fund managers use to evaluate and celebrate your work.

Live data

Deal room

A dedicated space to manage fundraising conversations. Share your deck, your diligence materials, and your financials with specific investors — privately, securely, and without juggling email chains.

How it works

From first profile to aligned capital,
without the guesswork.

01
Define what you're building
Start with your work — your mission, your community, your vision. Blueprint helps you articulate what you're actually doing in terms funders can evaluate.
02
Map what you need and when
Figure out how much capital you need, what type fits, and what timeline makes sense — grants, loans, recoverable grants, or equity.
